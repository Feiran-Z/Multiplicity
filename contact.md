<div class="content-block">
  <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

  <div class="content-faq">
    <div class="content-faq-section">
      <h3>General Questions</h3>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: Who is this website for?</h4>
        <p>A: Students, researchers, educators, and practitioners interested in causal inference methods. The content ranges from introductory to advanced levels.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: Is there a cost for accessing resources?</h4>
        <p>A: No, all educational content and resources on this site are freely available under open licenses.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: Can I use materials for teaching?</h4>
        <p>A: Yes! All content is licensed under Creative Commons (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0) for non-commercial educational use. Please attribute the source.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="content-faq-section">
      <h3>Technical Questions</h3>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: I found a bug or broken link. How do I report it?</h4>
        <p>A: Please use the contact form above or email directly with details about the issue and the URL where it occurs.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: Can I contribute content?</h4>
        <p>A: Yes! We welcome guest blog posts, resource suggestions, and code examples. See our <a href="/blog/#submission-guidelines">blog submission guidelines</a>.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: Is the website open source?</h4>
        <p>A: The website code is available on <a href="https://github.com/Feiran-Z/Multiplicity">GitHub</a>. Educational content is separately licensed.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="content-faq-section">
      <h3>Course Questions</h3>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: When will courses be available?</h4>
        <p>A: Courses are being developed in phases throughout 2024-2025. See the <a href="/courses/#enrollment-and-access">courses page</a> for the release schedule.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: Will there be certificates?</h4>
        <p>A: Yes, each course will offer a certificate of completion. A comprehensive certificate will be available for completing all four courses.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: Are there prerequisites?</h4>
        <p>A: Each course lists specific prerequisites. The first course requires only basic statistics knowledge.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="content-faq-section">
      <h3>Research Collaboration</h3>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: I'm working on a causal inference project. Can we collaborate?</h4>
        <p>A: We're open to research collaborations, particularly in educational content development or methodological applications. Please describe your project in your message.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: Can you review my research design?</h4>
        <p>A: We offer limited consulting on research design for educational or methodological projects. Please include details about your study and timeline.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="content-faq-item">
        <h4>Q: Do you offer workshops or training?</h4>
        <p>A: We're developing workshop materials and may offer training sessions in the future. Let us know if you're interested in hosting or attending.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
